祖母、心から
Grammie, from my heart

I wish in the temple of your heart
you would let me be the garden
where you ponder your sorrows.
I imagine the barren ponds filling--
it has been pouring, but the storm
is done now and the saplings
begin to emerge through
the tear-soaked earth a we sit
together in the peace of your temple.
