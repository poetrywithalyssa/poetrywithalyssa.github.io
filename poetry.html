I. SEASON OF WAVES: OLD TOWN, SWEDEN

My way to you was long, and there were no lights for me to harvest in the cities
below but rather, an unnerving amount of darkness, and when I reached you, the air
around me felt much like the cool of the cabin, except you had an air about
you that was dry, cobblestone paths connecting your modern veins to ancient thoroughfares
where great ships left your harbors to explore the unknown weaving in and out of your
archipelago, returning once new treasures were found then returned to castles about the city--those
castles became history and now your children learn as students in these ancient walls once
harboring great wealth in gold and sustenance but now in knowledge that will spread beyond
your borders and return to renew your land to continue building, higher until everything
returns back to you.
