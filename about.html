ABOUT

Alyssa Lo is an Asian American writer from Honolulu, Hawai`i. She graduated from Punahou School and currently studies at the Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City. Her primary literary interest is poetry, but Alyssa also enjoys history, science fiction, and creative nonfiction. Despite being allergic to cats, Alyssa is a keen cat enthusiast and would love to own a Russian Blue in the future.
