ABOUT

Alyssa Lo is an Asian American writer from Honolulu, Hawai`i. She graduated from Punahou School and currently studies at the Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City. Her primary literary interest is poetry, but Alyssa also enjoys history, science fiction, and mental illness narratives. Despite being allergic to cats, Alyssa is a cat enthusiast and would love to own a Russian Grey in the future.
